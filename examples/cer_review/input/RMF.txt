Risk Management File Summary

1. Risk Management Plan
Risks are identified, analyzed, evaluated, and controlled through the risk management process.

2. Risk Analysis
Identified hazards include electrical hazards, mechanical hazards, and use errors.

3. Risk Evaluation
Residual risks are evaluated against established acceptance criteria.

4. Risk Control Measures
Control measures include design controls, protective measures, and information for safety.

5. Overall Benefit-Risk Assessment
The overall benefit-risk assessment concludes that the device benefits outweigh the residual risks.

6. Production and Post-Production Information
Post-market surveillance data is collected and reviewed annually.

Reference: ISO 14971 risk management principles applied.
