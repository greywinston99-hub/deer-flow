Clinical Evaluation Plan

1. Objectives
The objectives of this clinical evaluation are to demonstrate safety and performance of the Example Device.

2. Scope
The scope includes all clinical claims and intended uses of the device.

3. Equivalence Assessment
Equivalence to the Predicate Device is claimed based on clinical, technical, and biological characteristics.

4. Literature Search Strategy
Search terms include the device name, intended purpose, and clinical outcomes. Databases: PubMed, Embase.

5. Clinical Data Sources
Data sources include published literature, post-market data, and registry data.

6. PMCF Plan
Post-market clinical follow-up plan includes safety surveillance and performance monitoring.

7. Acceptance Criteria
Acceptance criteria are based on clinical endpoints defined in this plan.
