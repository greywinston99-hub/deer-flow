Instruction for Use - Example Device

Intended Use:
The Example Device is indicated for diagnostic and therapeutic procedures as prescribed by qualified healthcare professionals.

Indications for Use:
- Cardiovascular diagnostics
- Treatment of specified conditions under specialist supervision

Contraindications:
- Patients with implanted electronic devices
- Pregnant women
- Patients with known sensitivity to device materials

Warnings:
- Do not use on patients with contraindications
- Ensure proper training before use
- Inspect device before each use

Precautions:
- Follow aseptic techniques
- Monitor patient during procedure
- Report adverse events

Storage:
Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
