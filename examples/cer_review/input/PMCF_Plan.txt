Post-Market Clinical Follow-up Plan

1. Objectives
The PMCF plan objectives are to:
- Confirm safety and performance in normal use
- Identify previously unknown risks
- Ensure continued acceptability of the benefit-risk profile

2. Methods
PMCF activities include:
- Registry enrollment of new patients
- Annual safety update reports
- Customer feedback monitoring
- Literature surveillance

3. Evaluation
PMCF data will be evaluated against pre-defined acceptance criteria annually.

4. Reporting
PMCF reports will be submitted to the competent authority as required.

5. Timeline
PMCF activities are ongoing throughout the device market life.
